Titanium Dioxide | TiO2

Titanium dioxide pigment (chemical formula: TiO2) is an inorganic white pigment found in an array of end uses. The most common use coatings and plastics accounts for more than 80 percent of global consumption.

Ellite Colorants markets the following range of titanium dioxide pigment grades.

01.

R-2195

The product is a Rutile titanium dioxide pigment with silicon, aluminium inorganic surface treatment, organic surface treatment, with high whiteness, good dispersibility, good tinting strength, high weathering resistance etc.

Learn More

02.

R-6618

R-6618 is a rutile TiO2 pigment produced by zirconium, aluminium inorganic and organic surface treated, designed to have average particle size distribution, good whiteness, high gloss, good dispersion, good hiding power and high weather resistance.

Learn More

03.

SR-240

SR-240 is an inorganic and organic surface treated rutile TiO2 pigment that is designed to deliver excellent gloss and hiding power, non-chalking and ease of dispersion in plastic and rubber.
